Understanding Fake News

Fake news refers to false or misleading information presented as legitimate news. It's designed to influence public opinion, generate clicks, or spread propaganda.

In today's digital age, misinformation spreads faster than ever before, making it crucial to verify information before believing or sharing it.

Misleading Headlines

Sensationalist titles designed to attract clicks but misrepresent the actual content.

Fabricated Content

Completely false information created to deceive readers.

Manipulated Context

Real information presented in a misleading context.
